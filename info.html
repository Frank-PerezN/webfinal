Maha's: A Hidden Gem of Egyptian Delights in Toronto

The Amazing restaurant opened since 2014 and it is run by 3 family members and their best friend. They have been thriving since then and the locals absolutely love them.

Indulge your senses in the enchanting aroma of sweet spices and savory delights that define the essence of Egyptian cooking. Maha's is not just a restaurant; it's a place where authenticity and craftsmanship are woven into every dish...

Address: 226 Greenwood Ave, Toronto, ON M4L 2R2. Accesible by Driving or by taking the subway + a 10 minute walk

I personally recommend people to try the Midblowing Chicken Sandwich, it is truly mindblowing!

Los Gyros: A Hidden Gem of Mexican Delights in Toronto

This hidden gem takes you on a culinary journey, where every dish is a testament to the passion and heritage embedded in every bite...

Savor the essence of Mexico through their meticulously crafted dishes and handpicked ingredients. Come and experience the heart of Mexico, right in the heart of the city!

Address: 816 Sheppard Ave W, North York, ON M3H 2T1 Accesible by Driving or by Bus.

I personally recommend people to try the Sirloin Tacos with Agua de Orchata its an amazing and delicious combo!
