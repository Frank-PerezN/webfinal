About

Hello, my name is Frank Perez, the creator of this website. I am currently about to start my second year of CPP at Seneca. Welcome to my digital showcase of Toronto's hidden gems!

I have a passion for exploring and discovering unique places, and I wanted to share my findings with fellow adventurers. Toronto is a city full of surprises, and my goal is to help you uncover awesome places that this vibrant city has to offer.

This website was created as part of the WEB222 course project. I took inspiration from various sources, including online travel blogs, local guides, and my own experiences. The design and content were carefully curated to provide an enjoyable and informative browsing experience.

Thank you for visiting my website and joining me on this virtual journey through Toronto's hidden treasures!
